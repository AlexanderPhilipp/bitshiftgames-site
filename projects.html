Drag Toss

Drag Toss is a Hyper-Casual Game developed for iOS and Android. The game focuses on having a high replay value with a simple but fun progression system. Thanks to the basic controls of the game, the player instantly understands the concept of the game. However the skill ceiling of Drag Toss is set pretty high since there are several ways to combine certain actions to master every obstacle and achieve a new highscore.
